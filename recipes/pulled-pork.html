Pulled Pork

Description

Whether you're planning food for a party, BBQ, or a simple weeknight dinner, this pulled pork recipe is a guaranteed crowd pleaser.

Serve it with BBQ sauce and your favorites side dishes, or serve it in sandwiches, tacos, nachos and more.

Ingredients

  • Pork
  • Onion
  • Your favorite BBQ sauce

Steps

  1. Dice the onion.
  2. Place the pork, diced onion and barbecue sauce in a slow cooker let it cook on low for 8-12 hours.
  3. When the pork is fully cooked and tender, use two forks to pull the pork apart and mix it around in the sauce
  4. Serve directly from the slow cooker! Enjoy!
